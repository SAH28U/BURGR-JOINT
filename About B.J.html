Our Story

Burger Joint is a beloved local burger outlet founded by the visionary Sandile Nyathi on 18 September 2025, in the heart of Sherwood Shopping Centre, Brakpan.

From humble beginnings, Sandile envisioned a place where the community could gather and enjoy expertly crafted burgers made from fresh, quality ingredients. The business is built on friendly service and a welcoming atmosphere for every customer who walks through the door.

Sandile’s hands-on approach ensures every burger meets his high standards. His passion for great food and community is the foundation of Burger Joint’s success.
